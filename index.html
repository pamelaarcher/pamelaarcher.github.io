About Myself

I am from Broomfield, Colorado a small bedroom community north of Denver.   I moved here 5 years ago from the California Bay Area with my spouse to be closer to family and to enjoy the great outdoors that Colorado offers including bicycling, snowboarding and incredible hiking.   I also love scuba diving and traveling to different world destinations.

For the past 25+ years, I have worked in the Healthcare industry leading technical teams in the design and delivery of complex software applications.   I love taking a problem and defining solutions to solve that problem.   It has been very rewarding to see direct outcomes of the work my team does and how that shows up in the final product.

Although this has been a very fulfilling career, I have recently chosen to take an early retirement to get back to my passion of hands-on web development, building beautiful but functional sites for non-profits and small businesses.   I am currently attending an MIT full stack web development certification program whose projects are featured on this site to bring my skills current and support this goal.

More About Me

Professional Summary

I joined Kaiser Permanente Health Plan in 2004 as a technical solutions consultant within their internal consulting organization responsible for Information Technology programs requirements gathering and technical designs.   I worked closely with business partners to deliver optimal technology solutions to address their Membership and Claims related business problems.   Over the next 18 years, I continued to support large systems replacement and enhancement programs through promotions to different leadership roles including Principal Solutions Architect, Senior and Executive Director where I led teams in the design and delivery of software.   These included multi-year programs such as the Medicare Modernization Act (MMA), the $850M Claims systems and $1B Membership replacement programs.

Prior to joining Kaiser, I worked for the St Alphonsus Regional Health System in Boise, ID as their Director of IT Planning and Development.  I led a team of analysts, developers and project managers in the delivery of healthcare solutions, working closely with physicians, patient focus groups, department heads and ancillary services.
